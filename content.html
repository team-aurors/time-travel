Time Travel

Time Travel means travelling into the past and the future. Before you learn about Time Travel you must know about Time Dilation.








Time Dilation

Before Einstein told everyone about the Theory of Relativity everyone thought Time is absolute which means Time is same everywhere in the universe. But then Einstein told everyone that Time is relative which means Time is different everywhere in the universe. The more the gravitional force of the celestial body the more Time is slow. This is known as Time Dilation. You might think that this is all a lie. In the 1970's an experiment was done to prove this. They had 2 clocks. They put 1 clock in the aeroplane and 1 one in the Earth. The aeroplane moved very fast. After some Time when the aeroplane came back to Earth they saw that the clock in the aeroplane was slightly slow than the clock of the Earth. This proves Time Dilation.
